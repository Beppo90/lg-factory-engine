## GLOSARIO MARÍTIMO POR UNIDAD (20 términos cada uno)

### Unidad 1: Ship Overview
bridge, engine room, deck, hull, bow, stern, port side, starboard side, container ship, bulk carrier, tanker, lifeboat, life jacket, fire extinguisher, gangway, mooring lines, hatch, hold, superstructure, propeller

### Unidad 2: The Crew
captain (master), chief officer, second officer, third officer, chief engineer, bosun, able seaman (AB), ordinary seaman (OS), deck department, engine department, watch-keeping, safety rounds, muster roll, crew list, command, maintain, navigate, secure, report, inspect

### Unidad 3: Places in Port
quay, berth, pier, jetty, dry dock, terminal, crane, gantry crane, container yard, anchorage area, navigation channel, approach, berth (v), mooring, unmooring, alongside, buoy, beacon, VTS, gangway

### Unidad 4: In Port
loading, unloading, stowage, lashing, TEU, bulk cargo, containerized cargo, hazardous cargo, bill of lading, cargo manifest, customs declaration, port clearance, pilotage, towage, bunkering, port regulations, ISPS code, crane (gantry), hold, hatch

### Unidad 5: IMO SMCP
MAYDAY, PAN PAN, SECURITÉ, distress, urgency, VHF radio, channel 16, position report, course alteration, heading, speed, anchorage, man overboard (MOB), fire drill, abandon ship, pilot, tugboat, VTS, COLREGS, GMDSS

2. **Unidades:** 5 unidades (no macrotemáticas), numeradas 1-5
3. **Código del programa:** Pendiente de asignación por SENA — usar [PENDIENTE] hasta que se defina
4. **Competencia y RAP:** Pendiente de diseño curricular formal — usar [PENDIENTE] hasta que se defina

6. **Fuentes auténticas:** Solo fuentes marítimas reales (IMO, shipping news, port authorities)
7. **Universo narrativo:** Caribbean Maritime Lines (CML), Cartagena, Colombia
