## ACTIVITY 3 — D: WRITING SCAFFOLDING — Build Sentences (15 min — Individual)

### STEP 1: Grammar Formula

```
DEMONSTRATIVES:
  This + is + singular noun  →  "This is the bridge."
  That + is + singular noun  →  "That is the engine room."
  These + are + plural noun  →  "These are lifeboats."
  Those + are + plural noun  →  "Those are mooring lines."

VERB TO BE:
  Subject + is + complement  →  "The hull is steel."
  Subject + are + complement →  "The crew are on deck."
```
