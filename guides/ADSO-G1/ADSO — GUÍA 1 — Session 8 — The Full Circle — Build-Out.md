## SET-UP DETALLADO (15 min) — 0:00 a 0:15

### Warm-up: "One Word" (10 min)

**Instrucciones paso a paso:**
1. Los aprendices entran. Instructor dice:
*"Good morning everyone. Today is the LAST session. Let's start with something simple."*

2. *"I want every person in this room to say ONE word in English that you learned in this guide. No repeats — every word must be different. I'll start."*
3. Instructor dice: *"Compatible."* Escribe en ONE WORD ZONE.
4. *"Next person — one word. Go."*
5. Ir alrededor del salón, persona por persona. Cada uno dice una palabra. Instructor escribe en el tablero.
6. Si alguien no puede pensar en una palabra única: *"Look at the board — what's NOT there?"* O *"What's a word you used in your Tech Request?"*
7. Continuar hasta que todos hayan hablado (o hasta que se acaben las palabras).

💡 *Esta actividad puede tomar 5-10 minutos dependiendo del tamaño de la clase. Con 20+ aprendices, puede ser largo. Si la clase es grande, hacer en grupos de 4 primero (cada grupo dice 4 palabras únicas), luego recoger las más interesantes en plenaria.*

8. *"Look at the board. ALL those words. YOU learned them. In 8 sessions. That's real."*

### Teacher Talk — Opening Script (5 min)

*"Today is the last session. Session 8. The Full Circle. We started with a question: 'What is the most important part of a computer?' And a controversy: 'Can you call yourself a developer if you don't know the parts in English?'*

*Today we close the circle. We review. We reflect. And you tell me — what did this guide give you? What didn't it give you? What would you change?*

*Let's go."*

### Objective (escrito en tablero)

*"Today you will: review everything, reflect on your growth, and give feedback on this guide."*

## WHILE DETALLADO (150 min) — 0:15 a 2:45

---

### BLOQUE A — VOCABULARY & GRAMMAR RECOVERY (40 min) — 0:15 a 0:55

**Agrupación:** Plenary → Teams → Individual → Plenary
**Objetivo del bloque:** Los aprendices demuestran retención del vocabulario y gramática a través de actividades rápidas de recuperación.

---

#### Activity 1: Component Identification (10 min) — 0:15 a 0:25

**Instrucciones paso a paso:**
1. Proyectar slide 35 (imágenes de componentes hardware — sin etiquetas).
2. *"I point — you say the word AND use it in a sentence. Fast."*
3. Instructor señala cada imagen. Un voluntario dice el nombre + una oración.
4. 15-20 imágenes. Ritmo: 30 segundos por imagen.

💡 *Si nadie sabe el nombre: dar la primera letra. "Starts with M... it shows you the image." → Monitor.*

---

#### Activity 2: Grammar Relay (15 min) — 0:25 a 0:40

**Instrucciones paso a paso:**
1. *"Form teams of 4."* — 1 minuto.
2. *"Each team gets a blank paper. Write as many CORRECT sentences as you can using To Be, Have/Has, and Demonstratives. All about hardware. You have 5 minutes."*
3. Timer: 5 minutos. Teams.
4. *"Stop. Count your sentences."*
5. *"The team with the MOST correct sentences wins."*
6. Teams leen sus mejores 3 oraciones. Instructor verifica gramática públicamente.
7. Celebrar al equipo ganador.

---

#### Activity 3: Pronunciation Check (15 min) — 0:40 a 0:55

**Instrucciones paso a paso:**
1. *"5 hard words from Session 4. Let's see if you still say them correctly."*
2. Las 5 palabras: Compatible, Processor, Ethernet, Gigabyte, Hardware.
3. *"Compatible — everyone together."* — Coro. Instructor corrige si es necesario.
4. Repetir para las 5 palabras.
5. *"Now — 3 volunteers to say the IT report from Session 4 (Activity 3) from memory."* — 3 voluntarios recitan el reporte del IT Specialist.
6. Instructor da feedback positivo: *"Great pronunciation on 'compatible.' Much better than Session 4."*

**Transition → BREAK:**
*"Take a break. 15 minutes. Come back to see how far you've come."*

---

### BREAK — Stretch & Recharge (15 min) — 0:55 a 1:10

Instructor prepara:
- Los resultados del quiz (si los tiene listos — para entregar o discutir)
- Los Blind Spots originales del tablero de Session 1 (si los tiene en foto/nota)

### BLOQUE B — GAP ANALYSIS REVISITED (50 min) — 1:10 a 2:00

**Worksheet:** PM-2.2 (original de Session 1 — cada aprendiz trae el suyo)
**Agrupación:** Plenary → Individual → Pairs → Plenary
**Objetivo del bloque:** Los aprendices comparan su punto de partida (Session 1) con su estado actual (Session 8), celebrando el crecimiento concreto.

---

#### Transición (5 min) — 1:10 a 1:15

**Teacher Talk — Transition:**
*"Welcome back. Take out your PM-2.2 worksheet — the Gap Analysis from Session 1. You wrote your Blind Spots. You signed a Learning Contract. Let's see: did you keep your promise?"*

---

#### Blind Spots Revisited (35 min) — 1:15 a 1:50

**Instrucciones paso a paso:**
1. *"Look at Activity 2 — your Blind Spots. The 5 situations. Can you do them NOW? For each one — mark ✓ if you can do it, or ❓ if you still can't."*
2. *"You have 5 minutes."*
3. Timer: 5 minutos. Individual.

**Las 5 situaciones originales:**
1. The name of the component that processes ALL information → CPU ✓
2. The name of the TEMPORARY memory → RAM ✓
3. "This computer TIENE 16 gigas de RAM" → "This computer has 16 GB of RAM" ✓
4. "The monitor IS a device for output" → "The monitor is an output device" ✓
5. The difference between SSD and HDD → SSD is faster, HDD is mechanical ✓

4. *"Compare with your partner. Did you both improve? How many ✓ do you each have?"*
5. Timer: 3 minutos. Pairs.

6. *"How many have ALL 5 ✓?"* — Manos arriba. Celebrar.
7. *"How many have 4 ✓?"* — Manos arriba.
8. *"How many have 3 or fewer?"* — Si hay: "That's ok. You still learned a LOT. Let's see what you DID learn."

9. Instructor hace el mismo ejercicio con la clase (como Session 1):

```
BLIND SPOTS — SESSION 1 vs SESSION 8:
                                           Session 1    Session 8
1. CPU name:                               [X] couldn't  [X] NOW CAN
2. RAM name:                               [X] couldn't  [X] NOW CAN
3. "has" sentence:                         [X] couldn't  [X] NOW CAN
4. "is" sentence:                          [X] couldn't  [X] NOW CAN
5. SSD vs HDD:                             [X] couldn't  [X] NOW CAN
```

10. *"Look at the board. From Session 1 to Session 8. THAT is progress."*

---

#### Learning Contract Completion (10 min) — 1:50 a 2:00

1. *"Now — Activity 3. Your Learning Contract. You wrote 3-4 things you needed to learn. Can you check them off? Write the 'completion status' next to each one."*
2. *"You have 3 minutes."*
3. Timer: 3 minutos. Individual.

4. *"Share with your partner. Read your contract and your completion. 2 minutes."*
5. Timer: 2 minutos. Pairs.

6. *"Who kept their promise? All items completed?"* — 3-4 voluntarios comparten.
7. *"Who has 1-2 items still pending?"* — Si hay: "That's your homework for life. Keep learning."

---

#### Celebration of Growth (5 min) — 2:00 a 2:05

*"One volunteer — stand up and tell the class ONE thing you can do now that you COULDN'T do 8 sessions ago."*

3-4 voluntarios comparten. Instructor celebra cada uno:
- *"You couldn't name CPU in English. Now you can describe a whole workstation."*
- *"You couldn't write an email. Now you wrote a Tech Request."*
- *"You couldn't have a phone conversation. Now you simulated a Help Desk call."*

---

### BLOQUE C — EXTENSION & APPLICATION (40 min) — 2:05 a 2:45

**Agrupación:** Groups of 4 → Plenary
**Objetivo del bloque:** Los aprendices integran TODAS las habilidades de la guía en un escenario nuevo que simula un reto laboral real.

---

#### The Challenge Scenario (5 min) — 2:05 a 2:10

**Instrucciones paso a paso:**
1. Proyectar slide 37 (The Challenge Scenario):

> *"You are at your first job. Your supervisor says: 'The new developer starts Monday. Set up their workstation. Write me the spec sheet and call IT if you need parts.' What do you do?"*

2. *"This is your FINAL challenge. It uses EVERYTHING you learned: vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, reading, writing, speaking. All of it."*
3. *"In groups of 4 — plan your answer. You have 3 things to prepare:"*
   - **(1) The Spec Sheet** — what components does the new developer need? Write a list with specs (in English).
   - **(2) The Phone Call** — if you need to call IT for parts, what do you say? Practice a 1-minute phone call within your group.
   - **(3) The Presentation** — present your plan to the class (2 minutes per group).

---

#### Group Planning (20 min) — 2:10 a 2:30

1. *"Form groups of 4. NOW."* — 1 minuto.
2. *"You have 15 minutes to prepare. Divide the work: who writes the spec sheet? Who practices the phone call? Who presents?"*
3. Timer: 15 minutos. Groups of 4. Instructor circula.

**Mientras circula, el instructor:**
- Ayuda a grupos que están bloqueados: *"Start with the spec sheet. What does a developer NEED? CPU, RAM, storage..."*
- Verifica que usan vocabulario y gramática de la guía
- Anima a que practiquen la llamada telefónica EN INGLÉS

4. *"5 minutes left. Finish your preparation."*
5. Timer: 5 minutos más.

---

#### Presentations (15 min) — 2:30 a 2:45

1. *"Who wants to go first?"* — 2-3 grupos presentan (2 minutos cada uno).
2. Después de cada presentación:
   - La clase aplaude.
   - Instructor da 1 comentario positivo: *"Great use of 'compatible' in the spec sheet." / "Your phone call was natural — good use of 'Got it.'"*

💡 *Esta actividad es celebratoria, no evaluativa. No hay nota. El objetivo es que los aprendices demuestren que pueden integrar todo lo aprendido en un escenario nuevo.*

**Transition → Feedback Loop:**
*"Excellent presentations. Now — I need YOUR feedback. This is the last thing."*

---

### BLOQUE D — FEEDBACK LOOP (15 min) — 2:45 a 3:00

**Worksheet:** Guide Feedback Form (1 por estudiante)
**Agrupación:** Individual
**Objetivo del bloque:** Los aprendices proporcionan feedback anónimo sobre la guía para mejorar la próxima ejecución.

---

#### Guide Feedback Form (15 min) — 2:45 a 3:00

**Instrucciones paso a paso:**
1. Instructor distribuye Guide Feedback Forms.
2. *"This is ANONYMOUS. Don't write your name. I want your HONEST opinion. This helps me make the guide BETTER for the next group."*
3. *"3 questions. Answer in English or Spanish."*

**El formulario:**

> **GUIDE FEEDBACK — The Hardware Specialist**
>
> 1. **What WORKED well in this guide?** (What helped you learn the most?)
> _______________________________________________________________
>
> 2. **What DIDN'T work?** (What was confusing, boring, or too hard?)
> _______________________________________________________________
>
> 3. **What would you CHANGE?** (If you could add, remove, or modify one thing — what?)
> _______________________________________________________________

4. *"You have 5 minutes."*
5. Timer: 5 minutos. Individual.

6. *"Fold the paper and put it in the box on my desk as you leave."* (O recoger directamente.)

💡 *El feedback anónimo es más honesto. NO lea los forms en clase — lea después para ajustar la guía para futuras ejecuciones.*

## WRAP-UP DETALLADO (10 min) — 3:00 a 3:10

### Teacher Talk — Closing Ceremony (8 min) — 3:00 a 3:08

*"Ok everyone. Eyes on me. Last words.*

*8 sessions ago, you walked in here. Some of you didn't know the word 'CPU.' Some of you couldn't write a sentence in English. Some of you were afraid to speak.*

*Now look at what you can do. You read a tech request email. You listened to a phone call. You learned 20 vocabulary words. You pronounced them correctly. You built sentences with grammar. You wrote your own email. You had a phone conversation in English. You took a quiz. You presented a workstation spec to the class.*

*That's real. That's yours. Nobody can take that from you.*

*The hardware English you learned here is the same English used in companies in the United States, India, Germany, anywhere in the world. The words are the same. The sentences are the same. The conversations are the same. You're ready.*

*Thank you for your work. Thank you for your effort. And remember — this is just Guide 1. There are 5 more guides in the ADSO program. Each one builds on what you learned here.*

*Well done."*

*"Class dismissed. Take your worksheets — they're yours. Keep them. Refer back to them when you need the vocabulary or the formulas."*

## DIFFERENTIATION NOTES

### Fast Finishers

- **Grammar Relay:** Si el equipo domina: *"Write sentences using COMPARISONS: 'An SSD is faster than an HDD.' 'This monitor is bigger than that one.' Bonus points."*
- **Gap Analysis Revisited:** Si completó los 5 ✓: *"Write a paragraph (5 sentences) explaining to a FUTURE student what they will learn in this guide. Use vocabulary from the Vocab Wall."*
- **Challenge Scenario:** Si el grupo termina la preparación antes: *"Add a BUDGET to your spec sheet. Research real prices. Present the total cost."*

### More Support Needed

- **One Word:** Si un aprendiz no puede pensar en una palabra única: permitir que diga una palabra que YA FUE DICHA por otro — el objetivo es participación, no exclusividad.
- **Grammar Relay:** Permitir que equipos usen las Grammar Formulas del tablero como referencia.
- **Gap Analysis:** Si un aprendiz tiene muchos ❓ restantes: enfocarse en lo que SÍ puede hacer. "Look at how many ✓ you have. That's progress."
- **Challenge Scenario:** Emparejar aprendices fuertes con aprendices que necesitan más apoyo dentro del grupo.
