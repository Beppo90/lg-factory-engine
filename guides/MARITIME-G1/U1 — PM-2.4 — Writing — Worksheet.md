### Survival Words:

| Word | Definition / (Traducción) |
|------|---------------------------|
| **checklist** | a list of items to verify / (lista de verificación) |
| **verify** | to check that something is correct / (verificar) |
| **located** | where something is / (ubicado) |
| **compliant** | following the rules / (que cumple las normas) |
| **deficient** | not meeting the standard / (deficiente) |
| **stowage** | how equipment is stored / (almacenamiento) |

**Action Required — Sample sentences:**

1. This vessel has 3 deficient items on the bridge and deck.
2. The crew must replace the EPIRB battery and 2 fire extinguishers.
3. The life rafts are expired — the service date is not correct.

### Description Paragraph — Sample (Bridge):

This is the bridge section of MV Caribbean Star. The bridge is located on the top of the superstructure. This section has 5 items of equipment. The VHF radio is on the bridge console. The EPIRB is on the port side of the bridge. The distress signals are in the bridge locker. The radar is on the bridge console. The magnetic compass is in the center of the bridge. These are 5 items of safety and navigation equipment. The inspector must check the EPIRB battery during the inspection.
