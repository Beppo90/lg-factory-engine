#### Task B: Analyze — Error Log (4 points)

**Error 1:**
- **(1) Error identified:** "The notebook have a pen" — incorrect verb conjugation (should be HAS, not HAVE)
- **(2) Corrected sentence:** "The notebook has a pen on the desk."
- **(3) Rule explanation:** "The subject 'notebook' is singular, so we use **HAS** instead of **HAVE**." (1 pt for complete analysis)

**Error 2:**
- **(1) Error identified:** "She am the receptionist" — incorrect verb form (should be IS, not AM)
- **(2) Corrected sentence:** "She is the receptionist at Global Solutions."
- **(3) Rule explanation:** "The subject 'She' is third person singular, so we use **IS** instead of **AM**." (1 pt for complete analysis)

**Error 3:**
- **(1) Error identified:** "These folder is new" — demonstrative mismatch (plural demonstrative with singular noun)
- **(2) Corrected sentence:** "This folder is new on the desk." (or "These folders are new on the desk.")
- **(3) Rule explanation:** "The word 'folder' is singular, so we use **THIS** instead of **THESE**." (1 pt for complete analysis)

**Error 4:**
- **(1) Error identified:** "The meeting room is next the elevator" — missing preposition (should be NEXT TO, not NEXT)
- **(2) Corrected sentence:** "The meeting room is next to the elevator."
- **(3) Rule explanation:** "The correct preposition is **NEXT TO** (two words), not just **NEXT**." (1 pt for complete analysis)
