## TEACHER FACILITATION NOTES

### Simulation Timing:
- Round 1: 5 min (Inspector A, Officer B)
- Round 2: 5 min (Switch: Inspector B, Officer A)
- Debrief: 2 min (What was difficult? What phrases helped?)

### Podcast Recording:
- Students record individually using phone/tablet
- Play 2-3 recordings for the class (volunteers only)
- Focus feedback on: demonstratives, chunking, clarity
- Do NOT focus on: grammar perfection, accent

### Common Errors to Watch For:
- ❌ "This is bridge" → ✅ "This is **the** bridge" (missing article)
- ❌ "The bridge is there" → ✅ "This is the bridge" (use demonstrative)
- ❌ "These is lifeboats" → ✅ "These **are** lifeboats" (plural agreement)
- ❌ Word-by-word reading → ✅ Natural chunking
