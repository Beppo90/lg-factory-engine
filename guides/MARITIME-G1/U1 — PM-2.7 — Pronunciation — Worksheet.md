## WARM-UP: Key Sounds for Spanish Speakers (3 min)

*These sounds are DIFFICULT for Spanish speakers. Practice each one 3 times.*

| Sound | Word | Problem for Spanish speakers | Tip |
|-------|------|------------------------------|-----|
| /ʃ/ | **ship** | Spanish speakers say "chip" | Lips forward, like saying "shhh" |
| /tʃ/ | **chip** | Different from /ʃ/ | Touch tongue to roof of mouth first |
| /θ/ | **starboard** | Spanish has no /θ/ | Put tongue BETWEEN teeth, blow air |
| /ɒ/ | **port** | Spanish speakers say "part" | Open mouth more, round lips |
| /aʊ/ | **bow** | Spanish speakers say "bo" | Slide from /a/ to /ʊ/ — "ba-oo" |
| /ɜː/ | **stern** | Spanish speakers say "starn" | Lips unrounded, long vowel |

**Minimal pair practice:**
- ship /ʃɪp/ — chip /tʃɪp/ — sheep /ʃiːp/
- port /pɔːrt/ — part /pɑːrt/
- bow /baʊ/ — bough /baʊ/ (same sound!)
- hull /hʌl/ — hall /hɔːl/

## PHASE 1: CHUNKING — Break It Down (10 min — Individual)

### THE CHUNKING RULE

*A natural sentence is NOT said word by word. It is said in CHUNKS — groups of 2-4 words that go together.*

```
❌ WORD BY WORD (robot voice):  "This / is / the / bridge"
✅ CHUNKED (natural voice):      "This is the bridge"  ← one chunk
```

**Chunking pattern for ship descriptions:**

```
[DEMONSTRATIVE + VERB + ARTICLE]  /  [NOUN + DETAIL]
"This is the"                      /  "bridge"
"That is the"                      /  "engine room"
"These are"                        /  "lifeboats"
"Those are"                        /  "mooring lines"
```

## PHASE 2: SHADOWING — Listen and Repeat (15 min — Individual or Pairs)

### THE SHADOWING RULE

*The instructor (or audio) says a phrase. You REPEAT immediately — same speed, same rhythm, same intonation. Do NOT wait for the instructor to finish. Shadow = follow closely.*

```
INSTRUCTOR: "This is the bridge ————————"
YOU (shadows): "This is the bridge ————————"
```

### ROUND 1 (3 min)

**TOUR GUIDE:** Point to each part. Say: "This is the _____." Use CHUNKS.

**VISITOR:** After each description, repeat back using a DIFFERENT demonstrative:
- Guide says "This is the bridge" → Visitor says "That is the bridge"
- Guide says "These are lifeboats" → Visitor says "Those are lifeboats"
