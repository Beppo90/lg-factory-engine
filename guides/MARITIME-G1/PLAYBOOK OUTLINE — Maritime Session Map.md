## NOTAS DE IMPLEMENTACIÓN POR UNIDAD

### Unidad 1: Ship Overview
- **Session 2 anchor:** Texto sobre inspección de buque o manual de partes
- **Session 3 audio:** Descripción de buque o recorrido por partes
- **Session 6 mission:** Vessel Inspection (PSC simulation)
- **Focus grammar:** "This is the bridge." / "Those are lifeboats."

### Unidad 2: The Crew
- **Session 2 anchor:** Artículo sobre vida a bordo o crew list descriptiva
- **Session 3 audio:** Entrevista a tripulante o briefing matutino
- **Session 6 mission:** Crew Briefing (presentation of department)
- **Focus grammar:** "The captain commands." / "Report to the bridge."

### Unidad 3: Places in Port
- **Session 2 anchor:** Port arrival report o mapa de puerto
- **Session 3 audio:** Comunicación VTS durante atraque
- **Session 6 mission:** Port Arrival (VTS simulation)
- **Focus grammar:** "The vessel is approaching." / "It's alongside, isn't it?"

### Unidad 4: In Port
- **Session 2 anchor:** Cargo manifest o reporte de operaciones
- **Session 3 audio:** Comunicación entre Chief Officer y Port Agent
- **Session 6 mission:** Cargo Operations (problem-solving simulation)
- **Focus grammar:** "There are many containers." / "You must check the manifest."

### Unidad 5: IMO SMCP
- **Session 2 anchor:** IMO SMCP document o emergency communication log
- **Session 3 audio:** VHF communications transcript o MAYDAY call
- **Session 6 mission:** Emergency Drill (full SMCP simulation)
- **Focus grammar:** "MAYDAY MAYDAY MAYDAY." / "If the engine fails, we must anchor."

## TEACHER TALK — FRASES MARÍTIMAS BÁSICAS

### Opening Cues (por unidad):
- U1: *"Good morning everyone. Today we learn the parts of a ship. This is the bridge."*
- U2: *"Welcome aboard. Today we meet the crew. The captain commands the vessel."*
- U3: *"The vessel is approaching the port. Today we learn where it goes."*
- U4: *"The ship is in port. There are many things to do."*
- U5: *"Today is important. We learn the words that save lives at sea."*

### Transition Cues:
- *"Ok, great work. Now let's listen to the audio."*
- *"Now turn to your partner. Check your answers."*
- *"Good. Now let's write."*

### Closing Cues:
- *"Before we go — what is one new word you learned today?"*
- *"Your homework is in the Workbook. Chapter [X]. See you next session."*
