## 📋 ACTIVITY 3 — B: SCENARIO — The PSC Inspector Is Coming (10 min — Individual)

*Read the situation. Answer the questions.*

---

### 🚨 SITUATION

> **You are the Chief Officer of MV Caribbean Star, a container ship.**
> Tomorrow morning, a **Port State Control (PSC) inspector** from the Autoridad Portuaria de Cartagena will board your vessel. The inspector will check:
> - The **bridge** (navigation equipment)
> - The **engine room** (main engine, generators)
> - **Safety equipment** (lifeboats, life jackets, fire extinguishers)
> - The **deck** (mooring lines, hatches, holds)
>
> The inspector speaks **only English.** You must show the inspector every part of the ship and name the equipment.
>
> **The inspector asks you:**
>
> *"Show me the lifeboats. Where are the fire extinguishers? Is this the engine room? What is this part of the ship?"*
>
> ⚠️ **If you cannot answer in English, the ship can be DETAINED.**

---
